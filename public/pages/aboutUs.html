Who we are?

LittleKingdom was founded in 2005.
We offer quality infant, toddler and preschool programs in Toronto area, that can be your child’s home away from home.We are dedicated to supporting children’s learning, health and well-being through our play-based early learning environments. We provide flexible hours and reasonable rate.
